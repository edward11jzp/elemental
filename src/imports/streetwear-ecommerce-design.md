Black & Dark Grey Streetwear Ecommerce Store + Admin Portal

Design a modern ecommerce clothing store inspired by premium lifestyle brands like U.S. Polo Assn., but with a bold, minimal, black-dominant aesthetic.

🎨 BRAND COLOR REQUIREMENTS (IMPORTANT)

Main colors:

Deep black (#000000 or near-black) as primary background

Dark grey / charcoal (#1C1C1C – #2A2A2A) for secondary sections

Medium grey accents for UI elements

White logo and white primary text

Very minimal accent color (if needed, subtle silver/grey tones only)

The design should feel sleek, bold, high-end, and minimal. No bright colors. No light themes. Dark modern UI throughout.

The site must include:

Public Ecommerce Store

Full Shopping Cart & Search System

Custom Product Upload Option

Pricing Logic

Admin Portal (Inventory + User Management)

Design mobile-first and fully responsive.
